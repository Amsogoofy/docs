## Welcome to Leafy

The Leafy bot is a professional verification bot that secures your community. It authenticates Roblox users via secure OAuth 2.0 and employs a custom CAPTCHA system for bot mitigation.

## Inviting Leafy

To ensure a high-quality experience and stable performance for all partnered communities, the bot's access is currently managed through a waiting list system. This deliberate approach allows us to provide dedicated support, maintain server integrity, and scale our infrastructure effectively. ## Why Choose Leafy?

Unlike public verification bots, Leafy maintains exclusive partnerships with carefully selected communities. This allows us to provide personalized support, prevent system abuse, and ensure the highest level of service quality for every partner server.

Whether you're managing a gaming community, educational server, or any Discord space requiring member verification, Leafy offers the security tools and dedicated support to protect your community effectively.

## What Leafy Offers

Our bot provides two powerful verification modules that can be used independently or together:

- **Roblox Verification**: Securely link Discord and Roblox accounts using official OAuth 2.0 authentication
- **Captcha Verification**: Advanced bot protection with guaranteed captcha challenges
- **Automated Role Management**: Smart role assignment and nickname synchronization
- **Selective Access**: Application-based waitlist ensuring quality server partnerships
